Our Story

Rooted in
Italian Tradition

La Piazza was born from a simple obsession: making pizza the way Nonna used to make it. We import our flour from Naples, use only wood-fired ovens, and let our dough ferment for 72 hours. The result? A crust that's crispy outside, airy inside, and utterly unforgettable.
